What We Do

RomeYork studio is a specialized video making service able to shoot any kind of situation for any kind of audiovisual formats: video clips, commercials, streaming live events, documentaries, and movies. The process is developed from zero to completion in all the different stages, from planning, through filming, editing and post-production. RomeYork studio has skills and expertise in all the required aspects of video making: direction, photography, editing and sound engineering. Moreover RomeYork is active on graphic design and visual communications, it develops and produces corporate images, branding, logo and any kind of printed material such as posters and brochures for commercial and advertising necessities.
